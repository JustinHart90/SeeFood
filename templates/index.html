DSI SeeFood

Problem

When your an avid food logger it can be difficult to stay on top of logging your meals when you are in a hurry. Sometimes you just don't remember everything that was in the meal.

Solution

The goal of DSI-SeeFood is to identify a user's image of a plate of food and return the micro nutrient counts for the identified dish. This is done by taking the closest related recipe image and returning its micro nutrient counts.

The Data

The data came from an API from a website called food2fork

With food2fork you make a request with a certain keyword and the json response contains the name, image url, and the micro-nutirents. We then take the image link provided and scrape the image off of the api.
